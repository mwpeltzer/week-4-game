Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four cyrtals below. By clicking on a cyrstal you will be adding a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total points to the random number - you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time a new game starts, the value of the value of the crystals will change.
